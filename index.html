Dressing Sustainably

Our main goal with our website, Dress Sustainably, is to raise awarness about the huge impact the fashion industry has on our environment and how much one piece of clothing can harm our beloved earth. By educating people using facts and statistics about how some textiles and fast fashion in particular negatively impact our environment and by giving some solutions to address these problems, we hope to make a difference and help people dress more sustainably and be aware of what they are buying as they can either be part of saving the earth or damaging it.
